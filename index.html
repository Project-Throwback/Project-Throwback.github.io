Welcome to Project Throwback

Project Throwback is dedicated to reviving the classic Fortnite experience from the early days. In 2024-2025, we're bringing back iconic features, old maps, and the community-driven spirit that made Fortnite the game we all loved. Whether you're here to relive the nostalgia or experience Fortnite like never before, we have something for everyone.

Join us on Discord to stay updated, meet fellow fans, and even get involved in community events and tournaments. We're committed to making this a place for Fortnite fans to come together and enjoy the original magic of the game, with modern-day improvements to enhance your experience.
